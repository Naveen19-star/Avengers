Avengers


The Avengers are a superhero team, kind of like Earth's mightiest protectors. They come from comics (originally created by Stan Lee and Jack Kirby in 1963) and have become super famous in movies too. The team is made up of different superheroes who join forces to fight big threats. Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Black Widow, and Hawkeye are some of the original members. They save the world from aliens, supervillains, and other big problems. There are even Avengers from different realities out there!

AVENGERS MOVIES

The Avengers


S.H.I.E.L.D. leader Nick Fury is compelled to launch the Avengers programme when Loki poses a threat to planet Earth. But the superheroes must learn to work together if they are to stop him in time.

Director: Joss Whedon
Release date: 27 April 2012 (India)



Avengers : Age of Ultron


Tony Stark builds an artificial intelligence system named Ultron with the help of Bruce Banner. When the sentient Ultron makes plans to wipe out the human race, the Avengers set out to stop him.

Director: Joss Whedon
Release date: 24 April 2015 (India)



Avengers : Infinity War


The Avengers must stop Thanos, an intergalactic warlord, from getting his hands on all the infinity stones. However, Thanos is prepared to go to any lengths to carry out his insane plan.

Director: Anthony Russo, Joe Russo
Release date: 27 April 2018 (India)



Avengers : Endgame


After Thanos, an intergalactic warlord, disintegrates half of the universe, the Avengers must reunite and assemble again to reinvigorate their trounced allies and restore balance.

Director: Anthony Russo, Joe Russo
Release date: 26 April 2019 (India)
